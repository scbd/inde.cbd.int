Please do not make arrangements for your side-event until you receive a confirmation that your side-event has been scheduled, and that a room has been assigned to it. Once confirmed, your side-event will be published in the side-event schedule https://www.cbd.int/side-events/. If your side-event is not approved, you will also receive an email with the details. You will recieve an email upon submission confirming your side-event request was received.

The deadline for editing your side-event is September 15th. You will recieve an email upon submission confirming your updated side-event request was received.
