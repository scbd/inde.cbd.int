How do I cancel my side-event?

Please email us at secretariat@cbd.int indicateing your cancelation request. Ensure the sending email matches the email of the requester/creater of the side-event request or the person indicated as the principal for the side-event within the form.

How to I change the title, description or image of my side-event?

  1. Go to your dashbord from the very top menu.
  2. Press the blue button "Go to Your Side-Events".
  3. Locate the side-event you want to edit and press the edit button on the far right of the row.
  4. Make the changes you want, then ensure to press the button "Edit Request" once finnished. This will send the change request to the Secretariat for approval. Once Approved the changes will be visible to the public.
